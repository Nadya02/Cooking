The Secret Ingredient:

Bananas!

Directions

STEP 1:

Peel 5 bananas and place them in a large ziploc bag. Freeze overnight.

STEP 2:

Once frozen, chop the bananas into small pieces and begin to blend. Blend until the bananads turn into a rich, creamy, silky blend.

STEP 3:

Try adding honey, chocolate syrup, maple syrup, peanut butter, flavored extracts, or additional frozen fruits, and enjoy!
