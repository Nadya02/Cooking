Berries and Coconut Cream

Ingredients

2 pounds mixed berries (such as blackberries and raspberries)

2 tablespoons granulated sugar

2 tablespoons fresh lime juice

1/2 cup cream of coconut (such as Coco Lopez)

1 cup heavy cream

Directions

STEP 1:

In a large bowl, gently toss the berries, sugar, and lime juice.

STEP 2:

With an electric mixer, beat the heavy cream and cream of coconut until soft peaks form.

STEP 3:

Serve with the berries.Enjoy!
