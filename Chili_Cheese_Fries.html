Chili Cheese Fries

"The spice will explode in your mouth, and make you drool! "

Chili Cheese Fries!!!

Ingredients

32 ounces frozen French fries

15 ounces can chili with beans

8 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese

2 green onions, sliced

STEP 1:

Bake French fries according to package directions

STEP 2:

While French fries are baking, heat the chili with beans in a medium saucepan set over medium heat, until heated through

STEP 3:

Top cooked French fries with chili and cheese; bake at 375 degrees for 8 to 10 minutes or until the cheese has melted

STEP 4:

Top with green onions
